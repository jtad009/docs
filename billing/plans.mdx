Flagmint uses **per-organisation flat-rate pricing**. Add your entire team — developers, product managers, QA engineers — without per-seat charges.

All prices are in EUR (€). All plans include EU data residency and GDPR compliance.

## What Counts as an API Call?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Billable">
    - SDK flag evaluation (REST `POST /evaluator/evaluate`)
    - WebSocket context update (each `{ type: 'context' }` message)
    - Flag management writes (create, update, delete flags/rules/segments)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Not billable">
    - WebSocket ping/pong (heartbeat)
    - Initial WebSocket connection
    - Health checks
    - Authentication requests
    - Failed requests (4xx, 5xx)
    - Read operations (listing flags, viewing settings)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Overage

If you exceed your plan's included API calls, overage billing applies:

| Plan | Overage Rate | Billing Unit |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Free | No overage — throttled instead | — |
| Pro | €4 per extra 1M calls | Full 1M blocks (rounded up) |
| Growth | €3.50 per extra 1M calls | Full 1M blocks (rounded up) |
| Custom | No overage — contract-based | — |

A **20% grace buffer** is applied before overage charges begin. For example, on the Pro plan (50K included), overage starts after 60K calls.

<Info>
  Free plan users are never charged. When you hit the limit, your SDK continues working with cached flag values — evaluations are throttled, not blocked.
</Info>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans at any time?">
    Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from **Settings → Billing** in the dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel my paid plan?">
    Your organisation downgrades to the Free plan at the end of the billing period. Your flags and data are retained, but features beyond the Free plan limits become unavailable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer a trial?">
    Paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a discount for annual billing?">
    Yes. Annual billing saves approximately 17% — equivalent to two months free. Pro: €290/year (€24/month). Growth: €790/year (€66/month).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored?">
    All data is stored exclusively within the European Union. See [Data Residency](/security/data-residency) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
